Installation

1

Open Chrome Web Store

Visit the Chrome Web Store and search for "Geo Helper - Ultimate" or click the link below.

chrome.google.com/webstore
2

Click "Add to Chrome"

On the Geo Helper extension page, click the blue "Add to Chrome" button. A popup will appear asking for confirmation.

3

Confirm Installation

Click "Add extension" in the confirmation popup. Chrome will automatically download and install Geo Helper.

4

Open the Extension

Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar and pin Geo Helper. Then click the Geo Helper icon to open the side panel.

5

Sign Up or Log In

Create a new account or sign in with your email and password using the form in the side panel.

6

Subscribe

Click the "Subscribe" button to unlock the coordinate capture feature. Complete payment through Stripe to activate your subscription.

7

Start Using Geo Helper

Once your subscription is active, you're ready to start playing! Open GeoGuessr and click "▶ Start" in the side panel to begin.

How to Use

📍

Login in

Ensure you are logged in to the right email and pass with an active subscription.

▶️

Start Capturing

Click the "▶ Start" button in the Geo Helper side panel. The extension will now monitor network activity for GetMetadata responses.

🎮

Play a Round

Play through a round in GeoGuessr. When you submit your answer, the extension captures the GetMetadata response containing the actual coordinates.

📍

View on Map

The extracted coordinates appear automatically in the side panel with confidence levels. An interactive map shows the location at your chosen zoom level.

🛑

Stop When Done

Click "■ Stop" to end capturing. Use "✕ Clear" to reset the display between sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Geo Helper safe to use? +

Yes. It's purely a study and learning tool for understanding GeoGuessr's data structures and improving your geography skills through legitimate practice.

Will this work in competitive matches? +

Yes.

What do the confidence levels mean? +

Confidence levels indicate how certain the extraction algorithm is about the coordinates. "High" means the coordinates were found in the standard location with proper validation. "Medium" means they were extracted but may require verification. Always cross-check coordinates with the map display.

How quick is it? +

It is as fast as your wifi and computer allow it to be. Tested on okay wifi and it shows the location before you have even seen where you are.

Do I need to pay for Geo Helper? +

Geo Helper requires a subscription to support ongoing development and server costs. You'll need an active subscription to use the coordinate capture feature. This allows the fastest speeds for our users.

What do I do if capturing doesn't work? +

First, make sure you have an active subscription. Refresh the page and try again. If the issue persists, get in contact with me.
